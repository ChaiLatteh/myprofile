DISCIPLESHIP TRAINING

Discipleship Training (DT) is a 20-week program designed to train, activate and equip you to live out your faith. There are four primary emphases in DT:
  • Part I: Identity & Freedom
  • Part II: Maturity & Character
  • Part III: Destiny & Storytelling
  • Part IV: Ministry & Outreach
DT is offered once a year, starting in January and ending in June. DT sessions are held for 3-4 hours on Saturday mornings. There are weekly assignments to be completed during the week. Participants are also required to attend a weekend retreat, complete service projects, and participate in a one-week outreach trip.
The course is open to anyone who has completed the Foundations course. If you are hungering to walk in the freedom that Jesus purchased for you, and seeking to live and serve as Jesus created and intended you to, DT will help you in the journey.
For any questions, contact Pastor Jonas Lee at jonasyhlee@gmail.com
