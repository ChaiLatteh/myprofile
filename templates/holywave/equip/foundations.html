FOUNDATIONS

Foundations is a 10-week course that explores the foundational teachings of the Christian faith. The course is split into two 5-week sections:
  • Part I (5 weeks): The Revelation of God
  • Part II (5 weeks): God's Work of Salvation
Foundations is offered twice a year, once in the Fall term (Sept-Nov) and once in the Spring term (Apr-June). Each week, the class runs for 2 hours on Sunday afternoons. The course will also require you to devote some time each week for assigned readings and reflection exercises.
