BAPTISM

We believe that baptism is a public declaration of our salvation and faith; it is an outward sign and a seal of agreement of the covenant we have with Jesus Christ.
Baptism applications are accepted on a rolling basis and include a personal testimony and interview portion.
Personal testimonies should be Â¾ to 1-page in length and address the following:
  1. What was your life like before you met Christ?
  2. How did you come to know Christ as your Lord and Savior?
  3. How has your relationship with Christ been transforming your life?
Once the application is completed and reviewed, applicants will be asked to have a one-on-one interview session with one of our pastors. The interview is meant to provide our pastors the opportunity to hear your story on a more personal level and have a conversation about the significance of baptism with you.
Please note that you must be a member of Sa-Rang Community Church to fill out this baptism application.
If you have any questions about baptism, please contact Pastor Jonas Lee at jonasyhlee@gmail.com. For all other questions, please contact us at holywave@sarang.com.
